March Madness Sadness

Every year my mom, my aunt, and I fill out March Madness brackets and trash talk each other throughout the tournament. Despite the small pool of participants, I haven't managed to win a tournament since 2009. This project is my attempt to explore and analyze college basketball tournament data with the goal of improving my future performance in the family pool.

ELO Ratings

ELO is a rating system initially developed to rate the ability of chess players. Each team starts the season at 1500. A team's score rises if they win a game and falls if they lose a game. Beating a high-ranked opponent causes a team's score to rise more than beating a low-ranked opponent. Mathematical details can be found at Wikipedia. For this application, I used a K value of 24.
